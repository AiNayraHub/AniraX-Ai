Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

🔒 Key Point: Your chat history is stored only in your browser using localStorage. We do not store your conversations on any server.

1. Information We Collect

At AniraX AI, we collect minimal information:

  • Chat Messages: Temporarily stored in your browser's localStorage for history functionality
  • Theme Preference: Dark/light mode setting saved locally
  • Anonymous Usage: No personal identifiers collected

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely for:

  • Providing AI chat service through Groq API
  • Saving your chat history locally for your convenience
  • Remembering your theme preference

3. Data Storage & Security

Important: All your data stays on your device:

  • Chats are saved in browser localStorage only
  • We cannot access your chat history
  • Clearing browser data removes all history
  • No server-side storage of conversations

4. Third-Party Services

We use Groq API to process AI requests. When you send a message:

  • Your message is sent to Groq's servers for processing
  • Groq processes the request using selected AI model
  • Response is returned to your browser

Please refer to Groq's Privacy Policy for their data handling practices.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser localStorage (not cookies) to save:

  • Your chat history (maximum 20 recent chats)
  • Your theme preference (dark/light mode)

6. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all data in browser DevTools → Application → Local Storage
  • Delete: Use "Clear All" button in sidebar to delete history
  • Export: Data is in your browser - you own it completely

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
