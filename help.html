❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is AniraX AI really free?

Yes! We use Groq's free tier which provides generous daily limits. No credit card required.

Which model has 2025 knowledge?

Qwen 2.5 32B has 2025 knowledge! Llama 3.3 70B has 2024-2025 knowledge. Others have 2024 knowledge.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Your chats are stored only in your browser using localStorage. We can't see your conversations.

Why is it called AniraX AI?

AniraX represents: Answers Intelligent, Rapid, Accurate, eXceptional!

Can I switch models mid-chat?

Yes! Your history saves per chat, but new messages use the currently selected model.

How do I clear my chat history?

Open the sidebar (☰) and click "Clear All" next to Chat History.

🔧 Troubleshooting

AI not responding?

  • Check your internet connection
  • Try a different model (some may be temporarily unavailable)
  • Refresh the page and try again
  • Wait a moment - Groq's free tier may have rate limits during peak times

History not showing?

  • Ensure your browser allows localStorage
  • Check if you're in private/incognito mode (disables storage)
  • Clearing browser data will remove history
