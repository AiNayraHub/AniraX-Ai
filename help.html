🚀 Getting Started

Welcome to AniraX AI! Here's how to get the most out of your experience:

💬 Start Chatting

Type your question and press Enter or click the send button

Click "New Chat" anytime to start a fresh conversation

🎯 Best Practices

  • Be Specific: Instead of "Tell me about Python," try "How do I read a CSV file in Python?"
  • Break It Down: Complex questions work better when split into smaller parts
  • Use Context: The AI remembers your current conversation
  • Verify Important Info: Always double-check critical facts from authoritative sources

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is AniraX AI really free?

Yes! We use Groq's free tier which provides generous daily limits. No credit card required.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Your chats are stored only in your browser using localStorage. We can't see your conversations.

Why is it called AniraX AI?

AniraX represents our vision: Answers Intelligent, Rapid, Accurate, eXceptional!

What model powers AniraX AI?

We use Llama 3.1 8B by Meta, running on Groq's ultra-fast LPU infrastructure.

Can I use this for commercial projects?

Please review our Terms & Conditions. For heavy commercial use, consider getting your own Groq API key.

How do I clear my chat history?

Open the sidebar (☰) and click "Clear All" next to Chat History, or use "New Chat" for a fresh start.

🔧 Troubleshooting

AI not responding?

  • Check your internet connection
  • Refresh the page and try again
  • Wait a moment - Groq's free tier may have rate limits during peak times

History not showing?

  • Ensure your browser allows localStorage
  • Check if you're in private/incognito mode (disables storage)
  • Clearing browser data will remove history

Dark mode not working?

  • Click the moon icon in the top right
  • Your preference is saved automatically
